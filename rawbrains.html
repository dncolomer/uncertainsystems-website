Raw Brains

Thinking is messy. Even the smartest people in the world sound uncertain, backtrack, get stuck, laugh at their own dumb ideas, and occasionally have genuine breakthroughs. That's the entire point.

Raw Brains is a weekly live experiment (X Spaces + screen share) where I invite heavy hitters from tech, crypto, AI, science, and beyond to solve the hardest problems they can find in public, unfiltered, in real time. No slides. No safety nets. No post-production polish.

It produces the highest-signal human cognition data on the planet, feeds directly into the GHC (Genuine Human Cognition) Dataset, and proves to the world that real intelligence isn't clean chain-of-thought. It's chaotic, emotional, iterative, and beautiful.
