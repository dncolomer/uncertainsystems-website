Our Vision

Educational Accelerationism

We believe in the radical acceleration of human learning capacity through open technology. Every tool we build is a step toward a future where knowledge flows freely, where learning adapts to the learner, and where the barriers between curiosity and mastery dissolve.

I've always been driven by the belief that education isn't just about acquiring knowledge—it's about accelerating human potential. In a world where technology evolves at breakneck speed, our learning systems must keep pace. That's why I'm investing in building open-source technology and AI to radically transform how we learn and teach humans.

Why This Stack Exists: Modern AI is overwhelmingly trained on finalized, edited, high-quality text—never on the live, uncertain, error-filled process of human discovery. By systematically collecting and structuring authentic cognition, we supply the missing signal needed to train and harness models that truly understand and augment human thought.

This is not just education. This is acceleration.

Our Core Open Tools

Five tools form our modular backbone for education acceleration

GHC

Human Cognition Dataset

Large open collection of real human thinking examples, gathered from people solving problems and explaining their thoughts out loud.

View on HuggingFace Pipeline
RAG

HC RAG Service

Vector database API that lets you quickly search and retrieve relevant parts from the Human Cognition Dataset for building accurate AI tools.

Coming soon
GHC

Genuine Human Cognition Score

A score that measures how closely AI Chain of Thought matches real human thinking patterns (using our dataset to detect fake or overly artificial responses).

Coming soon
oL

openLesson

Educational harness for LLMs. It turns any LLM into an optimal tutor for humans. It creates custom lessons, adapts to your progress, and implements the Socratic method for learning.

Try Alpha GitHub
oC

openCompanion

Tool to build and evolve educational AI companions that bring learning to an embodied experience in the real world.
