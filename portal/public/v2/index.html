The Science

A more holistic definition of knowledge.

Brain configuration is the full physical state at a point in time: neural path connections and connectivity, chemistry, and biological layers. It is everything that makes your specific brain the way it is, not just a logical representation.

Knowledge = Proximity

If your brain configuration is close in distance to someone who knows algebra, you know algebra. We need a map of brain configuration space, via medical imaging and analysis of many brains, to define regions of "knowing."

Learning = Transformation

Learning is the right trajectory from current configuration to target configuration. It is not always additive. Real learning can degrade other skills, like skiing interfering with snowboarding.

Read signals -> infer brain configs -> predict and optimize trajectories.
